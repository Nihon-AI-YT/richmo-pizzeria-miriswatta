The Oven

One clay dome. Built by hand, fed by wood, watched all night.

Our oven was built brick by brick in 2021 by a mason from Kandy who'd built exactly one before. It runs hotter on one side than the other — the pizzaiolo turns every pie four times so nobody can tell.

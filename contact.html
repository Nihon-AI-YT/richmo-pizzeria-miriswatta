Before You Come

A few things people ask

Do you take walk-ins?+

Always. Reservations just guarantee a table — walk-ins are seated as space opens up, usually within 15–20 minutes on busy nights.

Is there a vegetarian option?+

Yes — Marinara, Quattro Formaggi, Funghi e Tartufo, and the Polos Curry Pizza are all vegetarian.

Do you deliver?+

Takeaway is available at the counter, and we're on the major delivery apps under "Richmo Pizzeria Miriswatta."

Can I book the oven counter specifically?+

Yes, note it under seating preference — it seats up to six and is first-come after 7pm even with a note.
